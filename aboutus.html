What is Entrepreneurship?

Entrepreneurship is the process of creating, developing, and scaling ideas into impactful ventures. It’s about turning ideas into action — whether through startups, social ventures, or innovation within organizations.

The Role of E-Cell

E-Cell at MIET empowers students to think beyond academics and embrace entrepreneurial thinking. We support innovative minds through mentorship, resources, workshops, and networking opportunities.

Our Vision

To cultivate a thriving entrepreneurial culture at MIET where students actively explore business ideas, learn from real founders, and get hands-on experience in building ventures.
